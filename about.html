Currently a data analyst at Outplay Entertainment, a leading casual game studio I analyse game event data, research machine learning techniques, design A/B tests, and contribute to the implementation of the analytics infrastructure.

Previously achieved a PhD in Human Computer Interaction (HCI) at the University of Dundee. My thesis explored reasons why older adults are less likely to use social networking sites. A mixed-methods approach was used to firstly derive recommendations for designers of such sites, and secondly, to demonstrate the success of these recommendations on a working replica of Facebook. A list of publications from my PhD can be found here.

My professional interests include advancing my skills with the R programming language, experimenting with machine learning techniques, experimental study design, and understanding user behaviour.
